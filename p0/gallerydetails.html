Portfolio details

My 2D-styled artwork focuses on expressive characters, vibrant color palettes, and strong line work. I enjoy creating illustrations that blend stylized realism with a touch of fantasy, often exploring unique compositions and dynamic poses. My work ranges from detailed digital paintings to cel-shaded pieces with bold contrasts. I also experiment with textures and lighting to add depth while maintaining a distinct 2D aesthetic, semi-realism inlcuded. Whether designing characters or crafting immersive environments, I aim to bring emotion and storytelling into every piece.

In addition, I simply enjoy the pieces that comes together when it's finalized. I feel a sense of satisfaction when an artwork is complete.
