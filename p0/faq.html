FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions.

  1. Home
  2. FAQ
Do you sign your work? Yes, I do sign my artworks so they aren't stolen. I try not sign it in a bad place that might ruin the art piece but you can discuss with me about the placement for it when commissioning me.
Can you draw animals? I am not exactly the best but I can do my best on drawing animal features while still sticking to my art style. But no cars, I can't draw cars perfectly.
How did you find your art style? To put it simply, I just practiced a lot to get the style that I have.
Did you attend an art school? No, I'm a self taught artist but I considered on going.
How long does it take to finish an art piece? It depends really. I can take up to 2 or 3 days but for bigger commissions I would say a week.
